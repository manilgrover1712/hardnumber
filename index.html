How to Play

Find the hidden 4-digit NUMBER in nine tries.

Use the green and yellow indicators after each guess to see how close you are to the solution.

Examples

1 2 3 4
2 1

2 digits are in the correct spot, 1 more digit is present but in the wrong spot.

5 6 7 8
1 0

1 digit is in the correct spot. None of the rest are present.

9 0 1 2
0 0

None of the digits are present in the number.

A new Hardnumber will be available each day.

Note: All digits in the number are unique.
